Frequently Asked Questions

How do I record a tennis session?

Tap the microphone button at the bottom of the screen to start recording. Speak naturally about your session, and our AI will transcribe and analyze your reflections. Tap the button again to stop recording.

How does the AI coaching work?

CourtOracle uses advanced AI to analyze your session transcripts and extract insights about your performance, technique, and progress. The AI identifies patterns, tracks your goals, and provides personalized coaching recommendations based on your unique playing style and objectives.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes! Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices and store your data on secure Firebase/Google Cloud servers. We never sell your personal data. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your session data and notes. Contact our support team at kadenhartleyyokota@gmail.com to request a data export.

How do I delete my account?

Go to the Account page in the app, scroll to the bottom, and tap "Delete Account". You'll need to enter your password to confirm. This action permanently deletes all your data and cannot be undone.

What is HealthKit integration?

CourtOracle can integrate with Apple Health to track your tennis workouts, heart rate, and calories burned. This is completely optional and requires your permission. You can enable or disable this in your device's Health app settings.

How do I set and track goals?

Goals are automatically created by our AI based on your session reflections. You can view and manage your goals on the Home page. The AI tracks your progress and provides updates as you work toward your objectives.

Can I use CourtOracle offline?

You can record sessions offline, but you'll need an internet connection to process and analyze them. The AI transcription and analysis require cloud processing.

What languages are supported?

Currently, CourtOracle supports English. We plan to add more languages in future updates.

Is there a subscription fee?

CourtOracle is currently free to use. We may introduce premium features with subscription options in the future, but we'll always maintain a free tier.

Technical Issues

App Not Working?

If you're experiencing technical issues, try these steps:

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app
  • Check your internet connection
  • Restart the app
  • Restart your device
  • If the problem persists, contact support with details about the issue

Recording Issues?

  • Check that you've granted microphone permissions in Settings > CourtOracle
  • Ensure you have enough storage space on your device
  • Try recording in a quieter environment for better transcription quality
