Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 4, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to CourtOracle. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered tennis journal application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Profile information (name, tennis skill level, playing preferences)

Tennis Journal Data

  • Audio recordings of your tennis session reflections
  • Transcriptions of your audio recordings
  • Session notes, goals, and progress tracking
  • Performance analytics and insights
  • Opponent information you choose to track

Health Data (Optional)

  • HealthKit data (workouts, heart rate, active energy burned) - only if you grant permission
  • Physical profile information (height, weight, fitness level)
  • Injury history and health notes

Usage Data

  • App usage statistics and analytics
  • Device information and operating system version
  • Crash reports and error logs

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the CourtOracle service
  • Generate AI-powered insights and coaching recommendations
  • Track your tennis progress and goals
  • Personalize your experience
  • Send you important updates about the service
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed using secure algorithms
  • Data is stored on secure Firebase/Google Cloud servers
  • We implement regular security audits and updates

AI Processing

CourtOracle uses artificial intelligence to analyze your tennis sessions and provide personalized insights. Your audio recordings are transcribed and analyzed using OpenAI's services. This processing helps us:

  • Convert your voice notes into searchable text
  • Extract key insights about your performance
  • Generate personalized coaching recommendations
  • Track your progress over time

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google), OpenAI for AI processing, and other essential service providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of optional data collection (like HealthKit)

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we permanently delete all your personal data within 30 days, except where we're required to retain it for legal purposes.

Children's Privacy

CourtOracle is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by email or through the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@courtoracleapp.com
